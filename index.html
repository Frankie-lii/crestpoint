Welcome To Crestpoint Insurance Agency

As a duly registered and licensed independent insurance agency in Kenya by Insurance Regulatory Authority, we specialize in offering comprehensive coverage in life, general, and medical insurance.

At CrestPoint Insurance, we understand that insurance is more than just policies; it's about protecting what matters most to you. CrestPoint Insurance Agency is committed to delivering the best coverage solutions for individuals, families, and businesses.

In the unfortunate event of a loss, we offer unlimited support to our clients by guiding them through the entire claims process. Even in the most complex of situations, we advocate for our clients because our customers are our first priority - not the insurance companies we work with.
